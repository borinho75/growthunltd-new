How we work

Three movements.
One clear path.

Diagnosis to create clarity. Transformation to change something. Rhythm so it stays. Nothing cosmetic in between, and no slideshow.

Movement 01

Diagnosis

2 weeks honest look at your organisation. 4–6 deep interviews, a meeting rhythm at your side, hard separation between signal and noise. The result: a dossier that tells you something — even if you don't continue with us.

  • 4–6 deep interviews
  • Signal-noise analysis
  • Action options with trade-offs
  • Debrief with leadership team
Movement 02

90-Day Transformation

Implementation in the running business. We work as a duo, on-site and online, coaching, building, deciding with you. No PowerPoint recommendations, real interventions in organisation, rhythm and tools.

  • Operating rhythm installed
  • Workflows & tools live
  • Coaching on the job
  • Handover playbook
Movement 03

Operating Rhythm

The cadence after the transformation. A retainer model for transformation alumni — ongoing signal interpretation, tool maintenance and strategic sparring. You stay in motion without us becoming an obstacle.

  • Monthly sparring rhythm
  • Tool maintenance
  • Signal interpretation
  • 3-month notice
Stance

What we are. What we are not.

Are

Implementers

We go in. Get our hands dirty. We are in the room when decisions are made, and stay until it runs. Our success is measured by your independence afterwards.

Are

Craftspeople

We use modern tools, AI included, where they make a difference. Not because it sounds good, but where it eases real work.

Are

Entrepreneurs · Partners

We are not consultants but entrepreneurs who built companies themselves. Strategy and delivery from one head, proximity instead of account logic.

Not

Agency

We don't sell hour packages or build hypothesis decks. If you're looking for a marketing shop, you're in the wrong place.

Not

Classical consulting

No 80-page slide reports. No three-tier frameworks. No "recommendation to the CEO", we are with the CEO in the room.

Not

Innovation theatre

No hackathons for their own sake, no innovation labs without a link to the core business, no buzzword consulting. We touch the core.

Who we work with

Where we make the difference.

Our compass: we are strongest where decisions fall close to the helm and the organisation can really be moved.

01

Owner / Founder-CEOs, 30–300 employees

You're close enough to the helm to actually decide, and large enough that every decision has impact. Exactly our zone.

02

Existing product under pressure

The market is moving. The numbers are tipping. The assumptions don't hold any more. These are the moments where we add value.

03

Good companies that want to be ready

Not every engagement begins in crisis. Often it's about preparing a well-running organisation for the next change — before it arrives uninvited.

04

Leadership teams with ownership claim

We work with teams that want to remain responsible — not with those looking for an outside party they can hand decisions to.
