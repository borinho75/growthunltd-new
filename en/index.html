Principle 01

Implementation, not consulting

No recommendations to file. We are in the room when decisions are made — and build alongside.

Principle 02

Honest diagnosis

Before we move, we look. Paid, not gifted — an honest view is a product.

Principle 03

Duo, not pyramid

Two entrepreneurs who built companies themselves. No thinned-out junior team.

Principle 04

Tools instead of endless slides

Decks where they help. What stays are dashboards, workflows and decision frames.

Principle 05

Rhythm, not retreat

Two-day offsites move little. Change comes from repeated small acts in the right cadence.

Principle 06

In weeks, not quarters

Owner-CEOs decide on a weekly cadence. So do we.
