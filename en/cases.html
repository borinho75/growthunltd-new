Featured

INSCYD

Sport-science platform · International
Strategy · Goals · Team alignment

Context

INSCYD offers a platform for precise performance diagnostics in elite sport — strong data and methods background, plenty of energy in the team, but the challenge of aligning the business around a shared strategy.

Focus

Creating clarity on goals and strategy — and ensuring this clarity carries through to tasks and deliverables of the team.

Outcome

Within one year, a shared compass: the entire project aligned to one goal, the team running independently with the introduced tools.

"Boris helped us to gain clarity on goals and strategy and how this trickles down to tasks & deliverables. With his help, we aligned the whole project and team toward one goal. Don't get me wrong; it didn't become less work. But the work became more effective, fulfilling, and productive. Within one year, we changed for the better, and the whole team can now use Boris's tools and knowledge and run with it independently."
Sebastian Weber — CEO & Founder INSCYD
